What is the Down Well Diner?:


The Down Well Diner is the premeire midwest brunch spot to receive scratch-made, gourmet comfort food served to you over 100 feet underground! Located underneath gorgeous downtown Greensburg, Kansas at the bottom of the worlds largest man made well, we create the most savory and satisfying dishes reminiscent of down home Americana diners. Open for Breakfast and Lunch 7am-2pm, Wed-Sun.
